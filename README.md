# E-commerce Customer and Revenue Analysis

## Project Overview

This is an independent portfolio project based on a simulated e-commerce dataset. The project examines customer purchases, product performance, regional revenue, sales representative results, customer segments, payment status, and monthly business trends.

The analysis was completed using Excel, SQL, Tableau, and Power BI.

## Business Questions

* Which region generated the highest revenue and profit?
* Which products performed best in revenue and profit?
* Which sales representative generated the highest revenue?
* Which customer segment contributed the most revenue and profit?
* How did revenue and profit change from month to month?
* Which customers generated the highest revenue?

## Dataset

The project contains:

* 320 order records
* 80 customer records
* 10 product records

The data includes customer details, product details, orders, sales representatives, payment status, discounts, shipping costs, revenue, and profit.

## Tools Used

* **Excel:** Data cleaning, XLOOKUP, calculated revenue and profit columns, PivotTables
* **SQL:** Data import, table joins, revenue and profit calculations, business analysis queries
* **Tableau:** Interactive dashboards for regional performance, customer insights, product performance, and monthly trends
* **Power BI:** Business report pages and visual dashboards

## Key Insights

* The West region generated the highest overall revenue and profit.
* Air Fryer was the highest revenue-generating product.
* Maya generated the highest revenue among the sales representatives.
* April was the strongest month for revenue and profit.
* Customer 057 from the North Corporate segment was the top revenue-generating customer.
